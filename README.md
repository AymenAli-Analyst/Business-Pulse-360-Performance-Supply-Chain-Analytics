# 🚀 BusinessPulse 360: Supply Chain & Performance Intelligence Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview

Organizations generate massive amounts of operational and sales data every day. However, without proper analysis, identifying performance gaps, operational inefficiencies, and supply chain risks becomes difficult.

This project delivers an end-to-end Power BI dashboard that transforms raw business and supply chain data into actionable insights, helping decision-makers monitor financial performance, optimize inventory, improve logistics operations, and enhance customer satisfaction.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

The company faced several operational challenges:

* Fluctuating revenue performance across years.
* High percentage of late shipments impacting customer experience.
* Uneven sales performance across regions.
* Excess inventory leading to increased holding costs.
* Lack of visibility into warehouse and logistics performance.

Without a centralized reporting solution, management could not quickly identify risks or make timely data-driven decisions.

---

# 💡 Solution

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard that provides a 360-degree view of business performance and supply chain operations.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to:

✅ Monitor Revenue & Profitability

✅ Track Shipment Performance

✅ Analyze Inventory Levels

✅ Measure Warehouse Efficiency

✅ Compare Regional Sales Performance

✅ Identify Operational Bottlenecks

✅ Support Strategic Decision-Making

---

# 📈 Key Insights

### Financial Performance

* Total Revenue reached **50.20M**
* Total Net Profit reached **40.03M**
* YoY Growth achieved **19.66%**

### Supply Chain Performance

* Late Shipments reached **28.73%**
* Average Delivery Time was **5.49 Days**
* Inventory Value exceeded **199.52M**

### Regional Performance

* Riyadh generated the highest revenue.
* Dammam recorded the lowest revenue contribution.
* Significant opportunities exist for regional sales optimization.
